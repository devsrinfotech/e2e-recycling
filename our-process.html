How It works

Our Process
  • Extensive Network We have a nationwide network that allows us to collect e-waste from various locations, including businesses and households.
  • Easy Pickups Scheduled pickups are managed efficiently to ensure timely collection of e-waste.
  • Secure Transportation All e-waste is safely transported to our facilities in compliance with regulatory standards.
  • Material Segregation E-waste is classified into categories such as plastics, metals, glass, and hazardous materials.
  • Specialized Handling Each material type is treated according to its recycling potential and environmental impact.
  • Efficiency Segregation maximizes material recovery and ensures each component is directed toward its appropriate recycling process.
  • Data Protection Devices containing sensitive information undergo data wiping or physical destruction to ensure security.
  • Hazardous Material Safety Hazardous substances are identified and safely processed by certified facilities.
  • Compliance All security measures adhere to local and international environmental and safety regulations.
  • Plastics Recycling Shredded plastics are sent to authorised recyclers to be repurposed into new materials.
  • Metals Recovery Metals like aluminium, copper, and iron are melted down and transformed into usable metal ingots.
  • Glass Reuse Recovered glass is processed for reuse in various new products, preventing landfill waste.
  • Circular Economy Contribution All recovered materials are reintroduced into the manufacturing cycle to reduce the need for raw resources.
  • Comprehensive Reports Clients receive detailed reports outlining the volume and type of e-waste collected and recycled.
  • Sustainability Metrics The reports highlight the environmental impact, including reduced landfill usage and resource recovery rates.
  • Regulatory Compliance We help businesses meet their sustainability targets by providing transparent recycling documentation.
