Got any doubts in your head?

Read these FAQs and you’ll get answers for most of those questions.

At E2E, we accept a wide range of electronic waste, including old computers, mobile devices, printers, batteries, circuit boards, and more. If you're unsure whether your item qualifies, feel free to contact us for clarification.

Data protection is a top priority at E2E. We utilise industry-leading techniques for secure data destruction, ensuring that all sensitive information is completely erased before recycling. This includes physical destruction of data storage devices when necessary.

Yes, we offer pan-India logistics support for e-waste management. Whether you have one location or multiple across the country, our experienced team can coordinate pickups, transportation, and recycling to meet your needs.

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) is a regulatory approach that holds producers responsible for the entire lifecycle of their products, including disposal. E2E assists businesses in navigating EPR regulations by providing expert planning and implementation services, ensuring compliance with all relevant laws.

We pride ourselves on our prompt service. For intrastate pickups, we can often schedule a same-day collection. Interstate pickups are typically arranged within 24 hours, ensuring minimal disruption to your operations.

Once collected, the e-waste is transported to our certified recycling facilities. Here, it undergoes a thorough sorting process where materials are separated, and reusable components are recovered. Hazardous substances are safely disposed of, and valuable materials like metals and plastics are extracted for reuse.

Yes, E2E provides comprehensive documentation, including certificates of recycling and data destruction, ensuring that your e-waste disposal process meets legal and environmental standards. This documentation can be valuable for your records and compliance audits.

Before our team arrives, please ensure that all devices are unplugged and, if possible, remove any batteries or other accessories. You should also back up and delete any personal data from your devices. If you're unsure how to prepare your items, our team can provide specific instructions based on the type of e-waste.

E2E is committed to minimising the environmental impact of electronic waste. We adhere to strict recycling standards, focusing on resource recovery and reducing landfill waste. By responsibly recycling e-waste, we help conserve natural resources, reduce pollution, and contribute to a circular economy.

Absolutely! E2E specialises in handling bulk e-waste from corporate clients. We offer tailored solutions, including on-site assessments, customised collection plans, and dedicated support to ensure the efficient and compliant disposal of large volumes of electronic waste.
