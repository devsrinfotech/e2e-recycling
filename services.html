Deep dive into the services we provide

Comprehensive Electronics Recycling

E2E provides top-tier electronics recycling services for a diverse range of clients, including local and national businesses, other electronics recyclers, and electronics manufacturers. Our services ensure that your operations meet all state and national legislative requirements for e-waste disposal, while also contributing to a more sustainable future.
EPR Planning and Implementation

Navigating the complexities of Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) can be challenging. E2E offers expert assistance in strategizing and implementing your EPR plan, ensuring your business meets all regulatory requirements efficiently and effectively.
Pan-India Logistic Support

With our extensive logistics network, E2E provides comprehensive support for the collection, transportation, and handling of e-waste across India. Whether your operations are local or nationwide, we ensure that your e-waste is managed seamlessly and efficiently.
E-waste Collection, Storage, and Recycling

E2E takes charge of the entire e-waste management process, from collection and storage to recycling. Our eco-friendly practices ensure that your e-waste is disposed of in an environmentally responsible manner, meeting all legal standards.
Prototype Destruction

Innovation is the lifeblood of many industries, from technology and automotive to consumer electronics and medical devices. Protecting your intellectual property (IP) is essential, especially during the development phase when prototypes are often created and tested. At E2E, we understand the importance of safeguarding your proprietary designs and ideas, which is why we offer secure prototype destruction services. Our goal is to ensure that your innovations remain confidential and protected, helping you maintain a competitive edge in the market.
Data Sanitization

In today’s digital age, data security is a top priority for businesses of all sizes. When it comes to disposing of old or obsolete electronics, ensuring the complete erasure of sensitive information is critical to protecting your business from data breaches, identity theft, or unauthorised access. E2E offers state-of-the-art data sanitization services designed to provide businesses with peace of mind that their data is permanently and securely erased from all electronic devices.
